## What Is Oplinix?

Oplinix is a web hosting company with a Tier III-certified data center in Lagos, Nigeria. They're positioning themselves as a compliance-friendly host for businesses that need to keep data on Nigerian territory — particularly relevant as the Digital Nigeria 2030 national strategy puts pressure on government bodies, universities, and public institutions to host locally.

Beyond the compliance angle, they offer the full hosting stack you'd expect: shared hosting, WordPress hosting, VPS servers, reseller plans, business email, SSL certificates, and domain registration. The pricing is in USD, but they also display NGN equivalents.

## What Makes Oplinix Different From Generic International Hosts

Here's the thing most hosting articles won't say out loud: a lot of "Nigeria hosting" is just reselling servers from European or North American providers. You're paying local prices for performance that routes your traffic through Amsterdam first.

Oplinix actually runs a Tier III data center in Lagos. That matters for:

- **Latency** — Nigerian visitors load your site faster when the server is nearby
- **Data sovereignty** — compliance requirements for government, education, and healthcare institutions
- **Local regulation** — alignment with Digital Nigeria 2030 policies

They also throw in free website migration, which removes the friction of switching providers.
